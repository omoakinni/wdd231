About AIE Interiors

Our Story

Founded in 2015, AIE Interiors began as a small family business in Ibadan, Nigeria. Our founder, Akinlolu Ariyo, had a vision to bring high-quality, affordable furniture to homes and offices across the country.

Today, we've grown into a trusted name in the furniture industry, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission & Values

Quality Craftsmanship

Every piece of furniture is carefully crafted using the finest materials and attention to detail.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices and use sustainable materials whenever possible.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen to your needs and deliver beyond expectations.

Innovation

We continuously explore new designs and techniques to bring you the latest in furniture trends.
